Steak and Cheese Calzone

Ingredients

  • Shaved Steak
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Pizza Dough

Steps

  1. Turn pan to medium heat and add 1/2 stick of butter
  2. Add steak once butter is melted
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Add cheese when steak is cooked thoroughly
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  6. Stretch pizza dough out then add cheese steak to center
  7. Fold dough over cheese steak and place in oven for 10-15 minutes
