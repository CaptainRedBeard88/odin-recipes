Steak

Perfectly cook a delicious steak

Ingredients

  • Steak (preferably bone in ribeye)
  • Butter
  • Himalayan Sea Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Sage

Steps

  1. Let steaks get to room temperature
  2. Put a quarter stick of butter into pan set to medium and wait until it melts
  3. Add Steak to pan
  4. Add salt, pepper, a branch of rosemary per steak and a teapsoon of rosemary
  5. Let steaks brown on one side then flip
  6. Turn oven on
  7. Once steaks are browned on both sides put steaks in the oven and set oven to broil
  8. After a few minutes turn steaks over
  9. Take steaks out after a few more minutes
  10. Let the meat sit for a couple of minutes and serve
