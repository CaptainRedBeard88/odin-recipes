Chicken Tenders

Ingredients

  • Chicken tenders
  • egg
  • milk or cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • bread crumbs
  • corn meal or corn flakes
  • vegetable oil or cooking oil of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix eggs and milk or cream
  2. Add salt and pepper to mixture
  3. Mix bread crumbs with corn meal or corn flakes
  4. Add cooking oil to pan and turn to medium heat
  5. Soak chicken in egg mixture
  6. Move chicken from eggs to bread crumbs
  7. Wait until oil is hot enough to bubble when food is dropped into it to add chicken
  8. Flip chicken when bottom is brown. Time varies based on thickness
  9. Take out when both sides are cooked evenly and enjoy
